A Brief Inquiry To Online Videos

Me + Game = $$$

Online videos are a very... VERY popular form of media that anyone can make with little to no experience or fancy equiptment. Everyone has a phone with a camera that can go toe to toe with what many considered "high end" cameras 15 years ago. Today, I would like to share my thoughts on online videos, content creation, and it's effects on the brain. And when I say "the brain", I exclusively mean mine. I'm not a doctor, I don't know or care how it effects you. Do whatever you want.
